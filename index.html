RESEARCH

Examining a role for S100A12 in vascular inflammation

At BC Children's Hospital Research Institute, I worked to show that the protein S100A12 may be an indirect indicator of inflammation in primary vasculitis.

My findings were presented at the annual CBR (Centre for Blood Research) Research Day in 2017.

Escherichia coli O Antigen Serotype O16 Is a Restriction Factor for Bacteriophage T4 Infection

I worked with a team to demonstrate for the first time that O antigen, a ubiquitous bacterial polysaccharide, prevented infection by bacteriophage T4.

Our findings were published in the Journal of Experimental Microbiology & Immunology+ (JEMI+), UBC's undergraduate research journal.
